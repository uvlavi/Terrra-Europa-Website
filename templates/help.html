How-to Guides

Setup steps for the TERRRA Europa team site and email.

📤 Send mail as your @{{ domain }} address from Gmail

Each team member does this once in their own Gmail account.

  1. Open Gmail → Settings (⚙) → See all settings
  2. Click the Accounts and Import tab
  3. Under "Send mail as" click Add another email address
  4. Enter your name and your alias@{{ domain }} address
  5. Untick "Treat as alias"
  6. Click Next Step and enter the SMTP credentials below
  7. Gmail sends a verification email to alias@{{ domain }} — it will arrive in your personal Gmail (forwarded automatically) — click the confirmation link
  8. Done. When composing in Gmail, pick alias@{{ domain }} from the From dropdown

📥 Receiving mail at @{{ domain }}

How incoming mail reaches your inbox — set up once for the whole domain.

  1. The team domain MX records route *@{{ domain }} to a forwarding service
  2. Each team member's alias@{{ domain }} is configured to forward to their personal Gmail
  3. Organisation aliases like info@, sales@, receipts@ all forward to a shared inbox
  4. You receive mail in your personal Gmail with full content and links — and can reply normally

To add or change a forwarding rule — ask an admin. They'll add the alias and confirm where it should forward to.

✉️ Send mail from the team website

If you don't want to use Gmail, send directly from the site.

  1. Log in at {{ '/login' }}
  2. Click Compose in the top menu
  3. Fill in To, Subject, Message — your alias is set automatically
  4. Click Send. Replies will arrive in your personal Gmail.

⚙️ Domain-level setup (admin reference)

One-time setup for the whole domain. Only needed if migrating providers or adding a new domain.

DNS records at GoDaddy:
MX @ mx1.improvmx.com — priority 10
MX @ mx2.improvmx.com — priority 20
TXT @ v=spf1 include:spf.improvmx.com include:amazonses.com ~all
ImprovMX aliases (at app.improvmx.com):

For each team member, create an alias matching their username pointing to their personal email. Organisation aliases all forward to the wetlands inbox.

yuval@    → yuval.lavi@gmail.com
{user}@    → {user's personal Gmail}
info@     → wetlands.israel@gmail.com
sales@    → wetlands.israel@gmail.com
receipts@ → wetlands.israel@gmail.com
contact@  → wetlands.israel@gmail.com
* (catch-all) → wetlands.israel@gmail.com

Resend handles outbound (DKIM/SPF) for sending as @{{ domain }}. Domain is verified in Resend dashboard, no MX changes needed for sending.
